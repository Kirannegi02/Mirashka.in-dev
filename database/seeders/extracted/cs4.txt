Case Study: Boosting Employee Morale & Engagement at JYogi Technologies
Industry: IT Services & Software SolutionsClient: JYogi Technologies Private Limited
Background
JYogi Technologies Private Limited, a rapidly evolving IT services company, had built a strong reputation for delivering cutting-edge software solutions across industries. While the company’s client success rate was impressive, internal employee morale was dipping, and management sensed an invisible disconnect between leadership and the workforce.
With expansion plans on the horizon, leadership recognized that engaged and motivated employees would be critical for scaling the business efficiently. They wanted an unbiased HR partner who could dig deep, uncover real issues, and provide actionable solutions to rebuild a high-energy, motivated culture.
Business Challenge
Management at JYogi Technologies identified multiple signs of declining morale but lacked clarity on the root causes. Some of the key challenges included:
Low Employee Engagement: Employees seemed disconnected from the company vision, showing lower enthusiasm in team projects and communication.
Communication Barriers: Feedback rarely flowed upwards, leaving management in the dark about employee concerns and expectations.
Recognition Gap: Employees felt undervalued for their efforts, with limited avenues for recognition and rewards.
Unclear Growth Path: Employees lacked visibility into career progression, creating uncertainty about their future with the company.
Remote Work Strain: With a hybrid work model, many employees felt isolated and disconnected from the larger team.
Mirashka’s Customized Solution
Mirashka launched a 360° Employee Morale Revamp Program designed to diagnose, address, and sustain employee engagement. Our approach blended confidential insight gathering, leadership coaching, and tangible action plans to rebuild a culture of trust, inclusion, and communication.
Key interventions included:
✅ Confidential Employee Sentiment Analysis:Conducted one-on-one confidential interviews across all teams to understand real concerns, motivations, and cultural gaps—focusing on areas like leadership connect, recognition, workload, career visibility, and communication.
✅ Morale Health Scorecard:Built a customized Morale Scorecard, ranking various parameters like work satisfaction, growth clarity, manager relationships, and sense of belonging.
✅ Management Insight Report:Presented an unbiased, anonymized feedback report to leadership, along with prioritized action items that could deliver quick morale wins and long-term cultural transformation.
✅ Recognition & Appreciation Framework:Launched monthly recognition programs to publicly celebrate both performance and values-driven behavior, helping employees feel seen and valued.
✅ Leadership Engagement Training:Conducted focused leadership coaching sessions to help managers practice active listening, empathetic leadership, and transparent communication.
✅ Feedback Loops & Pulse Checks:Introduced quarterly anonymous employee pulse surveys, ensuring employees had a consistent channel to voice opinions.
✅ Career Pathing & Upskilling Roadmaps:Rolled out career progression frameworks mapped for every department, clarifying potential growth paths and skill development opportunities for each role.
Results Delivered
📊 Employee Morale Score Improved by 62%Six months after the intervention, internal morale surveys showed a 62% improvement in overall morale and engagement.
🤝 Leadership-Employee Connect StrengthenedEmployees reported feeling more connected to their managers, thanks to regular feedback sessions and leadership accessibility.
🌟 Recognition Boost:85% of employees cited better appreciation and recognition as a key factor in their improved satisfaction.
📣 Feedback Culture EmbeddedEmployees embraced regular feedback mechanisms, with participation in pulse surveys exceeding 90%, showing a renewed sense of ownership and trust.
🚀 Enhanced Retention OutlookTeam members indicated higher intent to stay, with 78% of employees seeing clearer career progression and enhanced development opportunities.
Conclusion
By partnering with Mirashka, JYogi Technologies not only diagnosed the root causes of low morale, but transformed its internal culture into a more transparent, appreciative, and future-focused environment.
The program not only resolved immediate morale concerns but embedded long-term practices that ensure engagement, communication, and recognition become core pillars of JYogi Technologies’ employee experience.
